Cuban Ropa Vieja

A fan photo of this savory Cuban dish

I have never made or tasted this Cuban dish, but if this recipe is any indication, it will be delicious. This slow cooked steak combined with a thick savory sauce is to die for.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 lbs beef flank steak
  • 1 cp beef broth
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Add veg oil to a large skillet and heat at medium-high heat. Then add the flank steak and cook until browned.

  2. Step 2

    Move steak to a slow cooker and then add tomato sauce and beef broth. Add tomato paste, bell pepper, onion, garlic, cumin, cilantro, olive oil, and vinegar. Stir until blended.

  3. Step 3

    Put the lid on and cook on low for max 10 hours. You may also cook on high for 4 hours.

  4. Step 4

    Shred steak and then serve. Should be served with white rice, black beans, and plantains.
