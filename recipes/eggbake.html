Bacon & Broccoli Egg Bake

Image courtesy of "yummytoddlerfoods.com"

A quick breakfast that will keep your kids fed and it is healthy to boot. Make it the night before and enjoy for days after.

Ingredients

  • 1 cp broccoli
  • 1/2 cp bacon
  • 6 large eggs
  • 3/4 cp all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cp shredded cheddar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Start by frying the bacon.

  2. Step 2

    Preheat oven to 350. Coat 8x8 pan w/cooking spray and line w/parchment paper.

  3. Step 3

    Mince broccoli and then add to med bowl w/other ingredients.

  4. Step 4

    Stir to make batter and then pour into pan. Bake for 22-24 min. Edges should be golden brown with a firm center.

  5. Step 5

    Let cool and then cut.
