Kraft Mac & Cheese

A bowl of "the cheesy stuff" courtesy of Wikipedia.

Have you ever heard the phrase, "You can't fix perfect"? That describes Kraft Mac & Cheese in a nutshell. A staple food of most American childhoods. This affordable dish is rich and filling and a welcome meal even in adulthood.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft Mac & Cheese macaroni noodles
  • 1 packet powdered cheese
  • 6 cups water
  • 4 tbsp margarine or butter
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Boil water in medium saucepan.

  2. Step 2

    Drain in colander over sink.

  3. Step 3

    Add margarine or butter, milk, and powdered cheese. Mix well and serve.
